Types of Amino Acids

Valine (Val, V)

Type:
Nonpolar (Hydrophobic)
Function:
Valine is essential for muscle metabolism, repair, and growth. It also helps regulate blood sugar levels and supports energy production. It is one of the three branched-chain amino acids (BCAAs) that are important for exercise and recovery.
Sources:
Valine can be found in high amounts in animal products such as meat, dairy, and eggs. It is also present in legumes, nuts, and whole grains.
Additional Info:
Valine plays a crucial role in the maintenance of muscle tissue and energy production. It also helps reduce muscle breakdown during intense exercise and supports overall recovery.
