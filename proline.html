Types of Amino Acids

Proline (Pro, P)

Type:
Nonpolar (Hydrophobic)
Function:
Proline is unique in its structure as it forms a ring structure with its side chain. It plays a critical role in collagen formation and protein synthesis. It also helps stabilize protein structures and can affect the rigidity of proteins.
Sources:
Proline can be found in high amounts in collagen-rich foods such as bone broth, chicken skin, and certain cuts of meat. It is also present in dairy products and some plant-based sources like soy products.
Additional Info:
Proline is important for the synthesis of collagen, which supports skin, joints, and connective tissues. It also contributes to wound healing and repair processes.
